Magnolia grandiflora, commonly known as the Southern magnolia or bull bay, is a tree of the family Magnoliaceae mative to the southeastern United States, from coastal Virginia south to central Florida, and west to eastern Texas and Oklahoma. Reaching 27,5 m (90 ft) in height, it is a large striking evergreen tree with large dark green leaves and large white fragnant flower, Widely cultivated around the world, over a hundred cultivars have been used commercially to mmake furniture, pallets, and veneer.
