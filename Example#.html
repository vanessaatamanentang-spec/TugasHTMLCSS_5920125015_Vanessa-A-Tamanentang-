Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has meltedd add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the eddge of the pan to the center 20 every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it willl continue to cook on the plate until served)
